As we have mentioned on How to Reduce Purchasing Cost on Cat Trees-Episode One, choosing a good manufacturer is essential. But how?

Large factory with huge area and lots of workers? It seems grant, but you cannot ignore the cost on management.

Or small workshop with traditional equipment? There is no one controlling quality.

Most buyer will tend to this type factory: it isn’t in large scale, but all things in order.

All new designs are tested by the Chief Experience Officer before mass production

Strict quality control system guarantees the defects goes nowhere;

Professional sale team can be reached at any time.

There is no specific definition of “good manufacturer”, it must be highly effective and work on solving all the questions from customers.
