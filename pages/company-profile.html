A professional manufacturer of Asia silver vine cat toys, cat teaser and Cat Tree, Sweet Pet Products(Beijing) Co., Ltd began in spring of 2014, With highly agreed understanding about pet industry trend, business principle, products development ideas between our team, Sweet Pet has been developing fast with great start of cat products, includes Asia silver vine cat toys, cat teaser wands and cat trees, then our designer team are the very known industrial designer in pet industry joined Sweet Pet due to the same passion and love for pets and developed various sorts and varieties cat products,We will provide more products and design services!

In the year of 2015, Sweet Pet established Sweet Pet Manufacturing center in Tianjin. China, where is about 1 hour’s drive to our head office in Beijing, with a great team includes designing, sampling, manufacturing and sales development, Sweet Pet is trying to offer more and more creative and high-quality pet products to the pets and their lovers worldwide. ODM is a big part of the business of Sweet Pet, during the past two years, Sweet Pet has become known by creativeness, reliability, high quality, high efficiency, and most important competitive Asia silver vine cat toys.

We are working with more and more leading companies in different countries includes Japan, Korea, Russia, and starts to cover North American and entire Europe. Sweet Pet always works with clients to onboard and protect their brand with high responsibility, we offer different products features for different clients which avoids unnecessary competition and helps to grow business sales for our esteemed clients, indeed we would like to call them Partners. To be different, to be fast, to be reliable, to be passionate is all members of Sweet Pet believe and have been dedicating to during the past years and will be in the long future, thus we believe we can accomplish our mission, “Just for Cats’ Sweet life”!
